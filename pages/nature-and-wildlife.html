Yala National Park

Yala National Park is one of Sri Lanka's most popular wildlife destinations. Roam the expansive park on a safari to witness a range of wildlife, from elusive leopards to herds of elephants. Enjoy guided tours and immerse yourself in nature.

×

Sinharaja Forest Reserve

Sinharaja is a lush, tropical rainforest and a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its rich biodiversity. Explore winding trails, listen to the sounds of exotic birds, and discover rare plant species in this natural paradise.

×

Horton Plains

Horton Plains is famed for its rolling grasslands, cloud forests, and the breathtaking drop known as World’s End. It’s an ideal destination for trekkers and nature lovers looking to experience Sri Lanka’s rugged beauty.
