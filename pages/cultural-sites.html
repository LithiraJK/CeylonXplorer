Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Sigiriya, known as the Lion Rock, is an ancient palace and fortress with impressive frescoes and landscaped gardens. It stands as a testament to the ingenuity of ancient Sri Lankan civilization.

×

Dambulla Cave Temple

The Dambulla Cave Temple is a vast complex of rock caves adorned with ancient Buddhist murals and statues. This UNESCO World Heritage Site showcases Sri Lanka's profound cultural and religious history.

×

Temple of the Tooth

Located in Kandy, the Temple of the Tooth is a revered Buddhist temple that houses a sacred relic of the Buddha. It plays a central role in the cultural and spiritual life of Sri Lanka.
