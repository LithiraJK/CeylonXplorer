Why Diyaluma Falls Will Enchant You

Diyaluma Falls enchants visitors with its tiered cascade and unique rock formations. The series of natural pools offers a one-of-a-kind experience, perfect for a refreshing dip amid nature.

  • Majestic Cascade — At 220 meters, the waterfall creates a mesmerizing, multi-level cascade.
  • Natural Pools — Its unique rock pools are ideal for swimming and relaxation.
  • Scenic Surroundings — The falls are set against a backdrop of lush greenery and rugged cliffs.

Best Time to Visit

The full splendor of Diyaluma Falls is best experienced during the monsoon season when the water flow is at its peak. The vibrant energy of the falls coupled with the cool mist creates an unforgettable experience.

Whether you're an adventure enthusiast or a nature lover, Diyaluma Falls offers both beauty and tranquility in the heart of Sri Lanka.
